About Us

At Kingsville Homes and Properties Ltd, we are more than a real estate company. We are a trusted partner in property management, building construction, and real estate solutions

Founded with a vision to redefine property services in Nigeria, Kingsville Homes and Properties Ltd provides seamless, reliable, and professional services to cater to students, professionals, investors, and families. We pride ourselves on delivering affordable housing solutions, modern construction projects, and efficient property management strategies.

Our operations extend across major cities, where we serve as a bridge between property owners and clients, ensuring transparency, value, and customer satisfaction. Whether it is managing residential and commercial properties, overseeing building projects from start to finish, or consulting on real estate investments, we bring expertise, innovation, and integrity to every transaction.

At Kingsville Homes and Properties Ltd, our goal is simple: To help people find comfort, create wealth through real estate, and build sustainable communities.
